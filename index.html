About Sadik International School

Nurturing excellence through innovative education in the heart of Mombasa

Welcome to Excellence

Sadik International School Tudor is a premier educational institution offering the internationally recognized Cambridge IGCSE curriculum for Years 8 through 11. Located in the vibrant Tudor area of Mombasa, we provide a world-class education that prepares students for global opportunities.

Our commitment to academic excellence, character development, and innovative teaching methods ensures that every student reaches their full potential. We believe in empowering young minds to become tomorrow's global leaders.
